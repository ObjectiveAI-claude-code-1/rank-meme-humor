1. Evaluate comic surprise by assessing how effectively the meme subverts expectations. Determine whether the meme uses irony, absurdity, exaggeration, or surprise to take the audience somewhere they did not anticipate. Measure the gap between where the viewer expects the joke to go and where it actually lands. A meme that detonates a familiar setup with an unexpected twist scores high. A meme that arrives at a predictable destination scores low. The wider and more delightful the subversion, the stronger the score.

2. Evaluate cleverness by assessing the intellectual craftsmanship behind the joke. Determine whether the meme demonstrates ingenuity in how it constructs its humor — through precise juxtapositions, layered meanings, creative recontextualization of templates, or visual-textual interplay that neither element could achieve alone. A clever meme rewards a second look and reveals depth beneath its surface. A meme that relies solely on brute-force randomness or shock without any underlying craft scores low. The more admirably constructed the comedic mechanism, the stronger the score.

3. Evaluate economy of delivery by assessing how cleanly and efficiently the meme lands its joke. Determine whether every element of the meme — text, image, panels, formatting — is load-bearing and necessary, or whether the meme over-explains, adds redundant context, or uses more than it needs. A meme that trusts its audience, leaves the joke implicit where appropriate, and delivers its punchline with surgical precision scores high. A meme that spells out what was already obvious, adds unnecessary captions, or uses three panels where two would suffice scores low. The leaner and more precise the delivery, the stronger the score.
